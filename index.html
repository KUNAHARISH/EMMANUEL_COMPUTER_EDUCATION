Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Our courses are open to anyone with a passion for learning. Basic computer knowledge is recommended for most courses. For advanced courses like PGDCA, a graduation degree is required. Please contact our admissions team for course-specific requirements.

What is the fee structure?

Course fees vary depending on the program's duration and complexity. We offer flexible payment options, including installment plans. Scholarships are available for deserving candidates. Please contact our administration for detailed fee information.

Are the certifications recognized?

Yes, our certifications are recognized by the industry and the Government of Andhra Pradesh. Our ISO 9001:2005 certification ensures that our training meets international quality standards, making our certificates valued by employers.
